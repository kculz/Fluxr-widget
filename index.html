⚡ Quick Start

Get started in 2 simple steps

1

Add the Script

Copy this before your closing </body> tag

<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/kculz/Fluxr-widget@main/widget.js" data-fluxr-public-key="YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY"></script>
2

That's it!

The widget appears automatically. Try it by clicking the button in the corner!
